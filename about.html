[ About the Founder & The Happening Story ]

Meet the Founder — Sarankarthick Santhi

A creator at heart, an engineer by degree, and a storyteller by destiny.
‍
Sarankarthick Santhi began his journey behind the lens at the age of 18 — driven purely by curiosity, emotion, and a dream to capture stories that truly matter. With a background in EEE engineering, he took the unconventional road and pursued his passion full-time, transforming a hobby into a calling.
‍
What started as a single camera and limitless imagination soon evolved into something greater. His early work earned trust, admiration, and a growing community of people who believed in his vision. This belief became the foundation for Montage Memories by Saran in 2020 — the first milestone in his creative journey.
‍
Sarankarthick’s philosophy is simple yet powerful:
“A story doesn’t just need to be seen — it needs to be felt.” This belief shaped every film, every frame, and every creative decision that followed.
