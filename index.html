"How to Plan a Perfect Outdoor Pre-Wedding Shoot"

A simple guide on choosing location, styling, golden-hour planning, and posing for outdoor pre-wedding shoots.

Choosing the Right Location

Outdoor shoots work best when the place reflects your personality.
Beaches, forests, or city rooftops — each gives a unique mood.

Golden Hour Is Your Friend

The soft light during sunrise and sunset makes skin look smooth and dreamy.
Always plan at least two hours before sunset.

Outfits & Styling Tips

Avoid too many patterns

Use pastel or neutral colors

Bring one flowy outfit for motion shots

Posing Made Natural

We prefer candid, movement-based shots instead of stiff poses.
Walking, spinning, talking, or laughing always produce honest expressions.
